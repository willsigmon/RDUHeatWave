# RDU HeatWave Meeting Structure

## Schedule

- **Day**: Every Thursday
- **Time**: 4:00 PM ET
- **Venue**: Clouds Brewing, 1233 Front St, Raleigh NC 27609 (side room)
- **Duration**: ~90 minutes

## Agenda Flow

### 1. Open Networking (4:00 - 4:15)
- Informal mingling, coffee/drinks
- Members greet guests, make introductions
- Lobby display (meet.html) shows QR code for guest check-in

### 2. Welcome & Call to Order (4:15)
- President or VP opens the meeting
- Brief announcements

### 3. Mentor Moment (5 min)
- A short inspirational or educational segment
- Rotates among members weekly
- Topics: networking tips, business wisdom, relationship building

### 4. Member Introductions (15-20 min)
- Each member gives a 30-second elevator pitch
- Focus on what kind of referral they're looking for this week
- "I'm [Name] from [Company]. This week I'm looking for [specific referral]."

### 5. Featured Presentation (10-15 min)
- One member gives a deeper dive into their business
- Helps the group understand how to refer for them
- Rotates on a schedule

### 6. Referral Round (15-20 min)
- Members announce referrals they're giving
- "I have a referral for [Member Name] — [brief description]"
- Tracked in Google Sheets for GI scoring

### 7. Guest Introductions (5-10 min)
- Each guest introduces themselves
- Members note potential fits for future referrals

### 8. BizChat Scheduling
- Members pair up for 1-on-1 meetings outside the group
- Goal: deeper relationship building

### 9. Closing & Next Steps
- Recap action items
- Confirm next week's featured presenter
- Adjourn

## Key Metrics

| Metric | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **GI (Gratitude Incentive)** | Points earned for giving/receiving referrals |
| **Steam Score** | Composite: referrals + attendance + BizChats |
| **Attendance %** | Weekly presence tracked per member |
| **Referrals Given** | Count of qualified referrals passed to other members |
| **Referrals Received** | Count of referrals received from the network |
| **BizChats Completed** | 1-on-1 meetings held outside regular meetings |

## Guest Process

1. Guest visits rduheatwave.team and fills out registration form
2. Data goes to Google Sheet (via Apps Script)
3. At the meeting, guest checks in via QR code on lobby display
4. Guest attends meeting, is introduced during Guest Introductions
5. After visiting, guest can apply for membership
6. Team votes on new member applications

## Roles

| Role | Responsibility |
|------|---------------|
| President | Runs meetings, sets agenda, represents chapter |
| Vice President | Assists president, fills in when absent |
| Secretary | Tracks attendance, minutes, referral counts |
| Treasurer | Manages chapter finances, membership dues |
| Member | Attends weekly, gives/receives referrals, completes BizChats |
