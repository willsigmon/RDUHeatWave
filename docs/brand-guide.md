### Design Principles

1. **Warm, not cold** — Dark theme uses brown/amber undertones, never blue-gray
2. **Fire as accent** — Orange is the only accent color. No secondary hue
3. **Animate with purpose** — Heat shimmer lines, flame particles, glowing orbs all reinforce the "heat" theme
4. **Respect reduced motion** — All animations disabled when user prefers reduced motion
5. **Print-ready** — Agenda page designed for exact letter-size output

## Voice & Tone

- **Confident but welcoming** — "Join the HeatWave" not "Please consider joining"
- **Action-oriented** — "CHECK IN" not "Submit your registration"
- **Heat metaphors welcome** — "Bring the heat", "Steam Score", "Boiling point"
- **Professional** — This is business networking, not a party. Warm and energetic, but grounded

## Content Themes

- Referral quality over quantity
- Trust-based relationships
- The extra degree of effort
- Growing together as a network
- Structured accountability (attendance, GIs, BizChats)
