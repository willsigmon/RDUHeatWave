What makes 212 different

RDU HEATWAVE

Two Twelve is built around the idea that the extra degree changes everything. In practice, that means a disciplined referral network where trust, credibility, and follow-through matter more than just showing up and swapping cards.

Why it exists

Founder Andy Fulghum built 212 around the boiling-point metaphor: 211 is hot, 212 creates steam, and that extra effort creates momentum. The whole network is designed around that “extra degree” standard.

What members actually do

  • Meet weekly so the room stays active, visible, and accountable.
  • Hold one-to-one BizChats outside the meeting to really learn each other’s business.
  • Make permission-based introductions they can confidently stand behind.
  • Protect category exclusivity so one trusted pro represents each seat.

The member standard

  • Referred business should be handled with exceptional service.
  • Follow-up is expected within 24 hours, not “whenever.”
  • Attendance matters, and substitutes help protect your seat.
  • Renewal is performance-based, not automatic, so value to the team matters.

If you know BNI

The weekly team rhythm will feel familiar. What changes here is the emphasis on gratitude, deeper relationship-building before referrals, and a tighter standard around who gets endorsed and how fast they respond.
